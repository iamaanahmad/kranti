# Code of Conduct

Kranti is a civic platform intended for respectful, lawful participation.

## Expected behavior

- Treat people with respect.
- Keep disagreements focused on facts and issues.
- Avoid harassment, threats, hate, doxxing, and intimidation.
- Respect privacy and survivor safety.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Abuse, harassment, or discriminatory language
- Encouraging violence or vigilantism
- Sharing personal information without consent
- Knowingly posting false or harmful allegations

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, restrict access, or take other action when behavior violates this code of conduct.
